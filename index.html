Matcha Grades Explained

Not all Matcha is created equal â€” some are a ticket to tea heaven, and some are best left for, well... less refined uses. Here's your guide:

Culinary Grade - The Green Imposter
  • Quality: Rock bottom
  • Color: Muddy brownish green
  • Good for: Baking, or anything where it can hide behind sugar.

Culinary Grade - Recipe Rescuer

  • Quality: A step above "meh"
  • Color: Dull green
  • Good for: Lattes, smoothies, and sneaking into recipes.

Premium Grade - Daily Sipper

  • Quality: Pretty solid
  • Color: Fresh green
  • Good for: Everyday lattes or a quick whisk in water.

Ceremonial Grade - Zen in a Cup

  • Quality: High
  • Color: Bright green
  • Good for: Whisking in water for that authentic Matcha experience.

Ceremonial Grade - Top-Tier Luxury

  • Quality: As good as it gets
  • Color: Vibrant, glowing green
  • Good for: Whisking in water for a full-on traditional tea ceremony.
